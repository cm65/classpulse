Support

Welcome to ClassPulse support. We're here to help you get the most out of our attendance tracking app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ClassPulse?

Download the app, sign in with your phone number, and you're ready to go. Create batches, add students, and start marking attendance immediately.

How do parent notifications work?

When you mark a student absent and submit attendance, ClassPulse automatically sends a notification to the parent's registered phone number via WhatsApp or SMS.

Can I edit attendance after submission?

Yes, you can edit attendance within 24 hours of the original submission. After that, records are locked for data integrity.

How do I add multiple students at once?

Use the CSV import feature in the Students section. Prepare a spreadsheet with student names and parent phone numbers, then import it directly.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your information.
